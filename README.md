# Online--Retail-Sales-data
Excel bases retail sales analysis project with cleaned data, pivot tables, dashboard, insights and business recommendations.
```markdown
# 📊 Overall Conclusion

The business demonstrates **strong overall performance**, generating **$10.6 million** in revenue from **19,960 invoices** while serving customers across **38 countries**.

## Key Insights
- 💰 Generated **$10.6M** in total revenue.
- 🧾 Processed **19,960 invoices**.
- 🌍 Expanded operations across **38 countries**.
- 📈 Sales peak during the **September–November** period, indicating strong seasonal demand.

## Challenges
- ⚠️ Revenue is highly dependent on the **UK market**, creating a concentration risk.

## Recommendations
- 🌎 Expand into international markets to reduce dependence on the UK.
- 📦 Diversify the product catalog to reach a broader customer base.
- 📊 Improve inventory planning to meet seasonal demand and optimize stock levels.
```
