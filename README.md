
# 📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis using Microsoft Excel

## Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end sales analysis workflow using **Microsoft Excel** on an Online Retail dataset. The project covers data cleaning, transformation, exploratory analysis, dashboard development, and business insight generation.

The primary objective is to transform raw transactional sales data into an interactive dashboard that helps stakeholders understand sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and geographical distribution of sales.

---

# Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Clean and prepare retail transaction data for analysis.
- Analyze sales performance across products, customers, invoices, and countries.
- Build an interactive Excel dashboard for business reporting.
- Identify sales trends and key business insights.
- Provide recommendations based on the analysis.

---

# Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional records from an online retail business, including invoice information, customer purchases, products, quantities, prices, and countries.

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|---------|--------|
| Total Revenue | **$10.6M** |
| Total Invoices | **19,960** |
| Average Invoice Value | **$533.171** |
| Countries Served | **38** |
| Domestic Sales | **98%** |
| International Sales | **10%*** |
| Peak Sales Period | **September – November** |
| Top Products | **DOTCOM POSTAGE, REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER, PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE** |

> **Note:** The listed top products together contribute approximately **47% of total sales**.

> **Metric Check:** The provided Domestic Sales (98%) and International Sales (10%) percentages are not mathematically complementary. These values are reported as provided and may represent different business measures. They should be verified before publication.

# Key Insights

- The dataset generated approximately **$10.6 million** in revenue.
- A total of **19,960 invoices** were analyzed.
- The average invoice value is **$533.171**.
- Sales were recorded across **38 countries**.
- Sales activity was strongest between **September and November**, indicating a seasonal increase in demand.
- **DOTCOM POSTAGE**, **REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER**, **PAPER CRAFT**, and **LITTLE BIRDIE** are the leading revenue-generating products, together contributing approximately **47% of total sales**.
- The dashboard enables quick exploration of sales performance across customers, products, invoices, and countries.

> **Important Note:** The dataset for **December 2011** may be incomplete. Therefore, any decline observed during December should not be interpreted as a confirmed decrease in business performance without additional context.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Monitor inventory levels ahead of the September–November peak sales period.
- Continue tracking the performance of the highest revenue-generating products.
- Investigate opportunities to increase sales outside the primary domestic market.
- Use customer purchasing patterns to support targeted marketing initiatives.
- Regularly refresh the dashboard with updated data to support ongoing business reporting.
